    Pronephros

    A precursor of the kidney that arises in the cervical region of the embryo during the 4th week of embryonic development and migrates caudally to connect with the pronephric duct (which eventually forms the mesonephric duct). Composed of nephrotomes and nephric tubules. Degenerates with the subsequent development of the mesonephros.
